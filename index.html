About Me.

I started my journey into design during my university life at United International University, where I studied Computer Science and Engineering and discovered my passion for creating meaningful user experiences. Now i am working a UI/UX Designer at Genesys Software. I have worked as a UI/UX Designer in companies like DIGIRIB, IQ Architects Ltd, InterioBD, and AR Group, where I designed websites, portals, and digital products across different industries. Some of my key works include designing an ERP HR Portal, Project Management Portal, Educational App, Restaurant Management System, CV Builder Platform (MakemyCV), Inventory and Accounting Systems, as well as travel agency and e-commerce websites. With each project, I aim to combine creativity and functionality to craft digital experiences that truly connect with users.
